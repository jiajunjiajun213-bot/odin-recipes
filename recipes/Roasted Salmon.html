Roasted Salmon's Recipe

People love roasted salmon for its buttery , flake texture and strong flavor

Ingredients

  • Salmon: 1 fresh fillet (about 150 to 200 grams)
  • Olive Oil: 1 teaspoon (or melted butter)
  • Lemon: 1 wedge (for fresh juice)
  • Seasoning: A good pinch of salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Turn your oven to 200°C (400°F) and line a small baking tray with foil or greaseproof paper.
  2. Pat the salmon fillet dry with a paper towel, rub it with olive oil, and sprinkle the top with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the salmon skin-side down on the tray and bake for 12 to 15 minutes until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
  4. Squeeze the fresh lemon wedge over the warm salmon right before you eat it.
